\need{Market Overview}

US equity markets advanced for the third consecutive week as
investors digested strong earnings reports from the technology sector.
The S\&P 500 extended its rally, supported by expectations of a
patient Federal Reserve and resilient corporate profit margins.

\need{Yield Curve Analysis}

The current yield curve shows a mild upward slope, having
normalized from the prolonged inversion of 2023--2024.
The 2s10s spread has returned to positive territory,
consistent with expectations for moderate economic growth
(\cite{adrian2013}).
